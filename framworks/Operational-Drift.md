# Operational Drift

## Working definition

Operational drift refers to movement away from expected process, data, reporting, control, or workflow behavior.

## Important boundary

Not all drift deserves escalation.

The relevant question is whether the drift is meaningful, credible, and actionable under defined operating constraints.

## Related questions

- What changed?
- Does it matter?
- Is it noise or meaningful drift?
- Has it crossed a threshold?
- Who should review it?
- What should remain human-owned?
