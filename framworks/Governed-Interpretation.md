# Governed Interpretation

## Working definition

Governed interpretation is the disciplined qualification of signals under defined rules, thresholds, review expectations, and accountability boundaries.

## What it is not

Governed interpretation is not generic detection, broad automation, or autonomous decision ownership.

## Core function

The function is to help determine what deserves credible attention under constraint.
